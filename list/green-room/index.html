Green Room

Green Room was far and wide my favorite movie of 2016, although that isn't saying much because 2016 had some pretty disappointing films.
It is about a punk rock band that witnesses a murder, and, as a result, gets trapped in a green room while neo-nazis try to break in and
kill them from the outside. It's pretty freaky.

Rating: 10/10
